Available Materials

Stainless Steel 304

Excellent corrosion resistance, suitable for most industrial applications. Ideal for food processing, chemical, and pharmaceutical industries.

  • • Good formability and weldability
  • • Resistant to oxidation and corrosion
  • • Hygienic and easy to clean

Stainless Steel 316

Superior corrosion resistance, especially against chlorides and acids. Recommended for marine, chemical, and high-corrosion environments.

  • • Excellent resistance to pitting and crevice corrosion
  • • Suitable for harsh chemical environments
  • • Higher temperature resistance

Mild Steel

Cost-effective solution with good mechanical properties. Suitable for non-corrosive applications with proper surface treatment.

  • • Economical and readily available
  • • Good strength and formability
  • • Requires protective coating for corrosion resistance
