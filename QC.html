Our Testing Procedures

Comprehensive testing methodologies to ensure product reliability and performance

Dimensional Inspection

Precise measurement of components using calibrated instruments including micrometers, calipers, CMM, and optical comparators.

  • Coordinate Measuring Machine (CMM)
  • Digital Height Gauges
  • Surface Plates & Granite Tables

Material Testing

Verification of material composition and properties through chemical analysis, hardness testing, and metallurgical examination.

  • Rockwell & Brinell Hardness Testers
  • Spectrometer Analysis
  • Material Certification Verification

Weld Quality Inspection

Comprehensive evaluation of weld integrity through visual inspection, penetrant testing, and ultrasonic examination.

  • Dye Penetrant Testing (DPT)
  • Ultrasonic Testing (UT)
  • Visual Weld Inspection

Pressure Testing

Hydrostatic and pneumatic pressure testing of tanks and pressure vessels to verify structural integrity and leak-proof performance.

  • Hydrostatic Test Pumps
  • Pressure Gauges & Calibrators
  • Leak Detection Systems

Surface Finish Evaluation

Assessment of surface quality, coating thickness, and finish consistency using specialized measurement instruments.

  • Surface Roughness Testers
  • Coating Thickness Gauges
  • Visual Inspection Standards

Functional Testing

Performance validation of assembled products under simulated operating conditions to ensure functionality and reliability.

  • Air Flow Testing for Filters
  • Assembly Fit & Function Checks
  • Endurance & Cycle Testing
