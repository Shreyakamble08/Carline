About Us

Welcome To Carline Auto Pvt. Ltd

Carline Auto Private Limited (CAPL) was incorporated in 1989. The Chairman and Managing Director, Late Shri. Ashok Sawant, was successful for 30 years supplying key sheet metal components and subassemblies to M/s Tata Motors Ltd, Mahindra & Mahindra Ltd., Matsushita, and Hatz Germany.

The company has developed proprietary components such as Air Filters, Air Cylinders, Air Tanks, Sensing Tanks, Shell Fuel Filter, and Lubricating Oil Filter.

Late Mr. Ashok J. Sawant, with extensive experience in the Automobile Industry, was earlier associated with M/s Telco and then started Carline Pressings Private Limited. The company has a dedicated workforce ensuring quality and efficiency.
