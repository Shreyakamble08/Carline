About Carline Auto

Precision Sheet-Metal Manufacturing Pioneers • Since 1989

Our Legacy

Carline Auto Private Limited (CAPL) was incorporated in 1989 under the visionary leadership of Late Shri. Ashok Sawant, a renowned technocrat with over three decades of excellence in supplying critical sheet-metal components to industry giants including Tata Motors Ltd., Mahindra & Mahindra Ltd., Matsushita, and Hatz Germany.

The company has earned recognition for developing proprietary components such as Air Filters (Telco 407 & exports), Air Cylinders for hydraulic brake systems, Air Tanks, Sensing Tanks, and deep-drawn parts like Shell Fuel Filters and Lubricating Oil Filters.
